No Student Left Behind, No Concept Too Difficult
Teaching must be about the student, never about the teacher. It is my genuine belief that the oft-feared subjects of mathematics and programming are accessible to everyone, if only presented properly. A growth mindset is essential, and can make the difference between a student who succeeds and one who gives in. That in mind, my philosophy is quite simple: take it slowly, acknowledge the little victories, and never give up on a student.
